About SAFARIH :

SAFARIH is an Egyptian shareholding company working in the field of public transport under the supervision of the Ministry of Transport since 2024.
It is the first private sector company operating in the area of public transportation.
SAFARIH provides technical solutions by using information and communication technology to improve passenger transportation services and works on developing
its benefits for the public to simplify their lives.

Our Vision :

Our vision is to provide an outstanding service that exceeds the needs and expectations of our customers to become the easiest and safest solution.
We always strive to become a leading company in the field of private transport services by providing comfort and luxury to all users of SAFARIH services.

Our Mission :

Our mission is to provide convenient and safe transportation services that our customers can rely on as a suitable alternative to vehicles.
Therefore, we follow our passengers’ needs to enhance our services. Our principle in safarih is the comfort and luxury of travelers.
