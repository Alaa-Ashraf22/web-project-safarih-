Welcome to our details page!

This is where you can find more information about SAFARIH.

SAFARIH is a leading online bus booking platform in Africa, serving customers in countries like Egypt.

The website provides a user-friendly interface for customers to search, compare, and book bus tickets for intercity travel.

Key Features:

1.Real-time seat availability and pricing.

2.Multiple payment options (credit/debit card, mobile money, etc.).


3.Secure transaction processing.

4.Downloadable e-tickets.

Booking Process:

1. Home Page: Customers can access the booking form on the homepage, which allows them to input their travel details (departure city, arrival city, travel date, number of passengers).

2. Search Results: Based on the entered details, the website displays a list of available bus options, including departure/arrival times, bus company, bus type, ticket price, and seat availability.

3. Trip Selection: Customers can select the preferred bus option and proceed to the checkout page.

4. Passenger & Payment: On the checkout page, customers enter the passenger details (name, contact information) and choose their preferred payment method to complete the booking.

5. Confirmation: After successful payment, customers receive a booking confirmation with a reservation number and a downloadable e-ticket.

Additional Features:

1.Route Maps: Interactive maps showing the bus routes and stops.
2.Bus Amenities: Information about the facilities and amenities available on different bus types (e.g., Wi-Fi, onboard entertainment, reclining seats).
3.Loyalty Program: Registered users can earn points for their bookings and redeem them for discounts or other benefits.
4.Mobile App: SAFARIH also offers a mobile app for iOS and Android, allowing customers to book tickets on-the-go.
5.SAFARIH's website is designed to provide a seamless and reliable bus booking experience for travelers in Africa. The platform aims to offer a wide range of bus options, competitive pricing, and excellent customer support.
